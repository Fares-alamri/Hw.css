أكاديمية طويق

Tuwaiq Academy is an educational institution based in Riyadh. The Saudi Federation for Cybersecurity, Programming and Drones announced its establishment in August 2019, and it is based in Noura bint Abdul Rahman University.It is the first Academy for Advanced Technologies in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, by providing a high level of training programs on advanced technology and camps in programming, software engineering, data science, and cybersecurity. It seeks to reduce the gap between the requirements of the labor market and the skills of individuals in the fields of information technology, Focusing on capacity building, providing new experiences, and developing skills. It works to provide the job market with digital professionals seeking corporate technology jobs.

Strategic partnerships

It is noteworthy that Tuwaiq Academy has held several partnerships with Major leading technology companies In the world; (Google, Amazon, IBM, Apple, Cisco, Alibaba Cloud, Oracle, Microsoft, Trend Micro, and Executive Security), where they will cooperate with the Academy In "launching their training programs" to benefit the various segments of society.
